## Partner Categories

### 1. Operating Partner (CRITICAL)

**Role:** Holds the licenses, runs the operations, brings deal flow

**Requirements:**
- PPRA-registered firm with valid FFC
- Trust account with IRBA auditor
- Property industry experience (5+ years preferred)
- Operational capability (staff, systems, networks)

**What they do:**
- Source investment properties
- Negotiate deals
- Handle compliance (FICA, disclosures)
- Manage trust account
- Coordinate tenant placement
- Oversee property management

**Relationship:** JV partner (50% equity)

**Where to find:**
- Established property management companies
- Senior estate agents going independent
- Property investment specialists

---

### 2. Legal Partners

#### 2.1 Conveyancing Attorney

**Role:** Handle property transfers, bond registrations

**Requirements:**
- Admitted attorney and conveyancer
- Valid LPC Fidelity Fund Certificate
- Trust account

**What they do:**
- Prepare transfer documents
- Lodge at Deeds Office
- Handle bond registration
- Manage transfer process

**Relationship:** Panel attorney (per-transaction fees)

**Where to find:**
- Established conveyancing firms
- Often come with operating partner relationships

#### 2.2 Property Law Specialist

**Role:** Contracts, compliance, disputes

**What they do:**
- Draft/review standard contracts
- Advise on regulatory compliance
- Handle disputes and litigation
- Sectional title matters

**Relationship:** Retained or ad-hoc

---

### 3. Financial Partners

#### 3.1 Bank

**Role:** Trust account, owner accounts, financing

**Requirements:**
- Must have PPRA arrangement for trust accounts

**What they do:**
- Hold trust account
- Process payments
- Potentially offer buyer financing

**Relationship:** Banking relationship

#### 3.2 IRBA-Registered Auditor

**Role:** Annual trust account audit

**Requirements:**
- IRBA registration
- Experience with property practitioners

**What they do:**
- Annual audit of trust account
- Submit report to PPRA

**Relationship:** Annual retainer

#### 3.3 Tax Practitioner

**Role:** Owner tax support, business tax compliance

**What they do:**
- Rental income tax advice
- VAT registration/compliance
- Capital gains tax planning
- Annual tax returns

**Relationship:** Panel or referral

---

### 4. Service Partners

#### 4.1 Tenant Screening

**Role:** Credit checks, verification

**Providers:**
- TPN (Tenant Profile Network)
- Experian
- PayProp

**What they provide:**
- Credit checks
- Payment history
- Blacklist verification
- Employment verification

**Relationship:** API integration or per-check fees

#### 4.2 Maintenance Network

**Role:** Property repairs and maintenance

**Categories:**
- Plumbing
- Electrical
- General handyman
- Appliance repair
- Pest control
- Landscaping

**Relationship:** Approved contractor panel (markup or flat fee arrangement)

#### 4.3 Cleaning Services

**Role:** Turnover cleaning for short-term rentals

**What they do:**
- Post-checkout cleaning
- Linen changes
- Restocking supplies

**Relationship:** Per-clean fees

#### 4.4 Insurance Broker

**Role:** Property and landlord insurance

**Coverage types:**
- Building insurance
- Contents insurance
- Rental income protection
- Public liability
- Short-term rental specific cover

**Relationship:** Broker referral (commission-based)

---

### 5. Short-Term Rental Partners

#### 5.1 Booking Platforms

**Platforms:**
- Airbnb
- Booking.com
- Expedia (VRBO)
- LekkeSlaap
- SafariNow

**Relationship:** Listing agreements, API integration

#### 5.2 Co-Host / Management Companies

**Option A: Partner with existing**
- Houst (14-20% commission)
- Propr
- HostAgents
- LaStay

**Option B: Build internal capability**
- Hire operations staff
- Build guest communication systems
- Coordinate cleaning network

**Recommendation:** Start with partnership, build internal capability over time

---

### 6. Platform Partners

#### 6.1 Payment Gateway

**Providers:**
- PayFast
- Peach Payments
- Yoco

**What they do:**
- Rent collection
- Fee processing
- Owner disbursements

**Relationship:** Integration partner (per-transaction fees)

#### 6.2 CRM

**Options:**
- HubSpot
- Pipedrive
- Zoho CRM

**What it does:**
- Lead management
- Deal pipeline
- Communication tracking

**Relationship:** SaaS subscription

#### 6.3 Accounting Software

**Options:**
- Xero
- Sage
- QuickBooks

**What it does:**
- Trust account reconciliation
- Owner statements
- Financial reporting

**Relationship:** SaaS subscription

| CRM | ✓ (expensive) | ✓ (mature) | **Partner** (buy) |
