# Keystay: Business Model

**Name:** Keystay
**Date:** 20 January 2026
**Status:** Business Design Phase
**Structure:** Joint Venture — Ampersand Insights + Operating Partner

---

## The Business

Keystay is a vertically integrated property platform that enables people to own residential property and earn income from it — without the hassle of being a landlord.

**Core proposition:** You get the keys. We handle everything else. Income flows to you.

## Service Lines

### 1. Property Sales

Source and sell residential properties to buyers seeking income-generating assets.

| Ownership Model | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Full ownership** | Buyer owns 100% of the property title. Traditional model with full control and equity. |
| **Fractional ownership** | Multiple buyers share ownership via SPV or similar structure. Lower entry point, shared risk. |

**Fee:** Percentage of purchase price (TBD)

### 2. Tenant Placement

Find and place occupants in properties to generate income for owners.

| Occupancy Model | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Long-term rental** | Traditional 12-month+ leases. Stable, predictable income. Lower management overhead. |
| **Short-term rental** | Airbnb, Booking.com, etc. Higher yield potential. More active management required. |

**Fee:** Percentage of rent or flat fee (TBD)

### 3. Property Management

Ongoing management of tenanted properties for hands-off ownership.

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Rent collection | Collect from tenants, disburse to owners |
| Maintenance | Coordinate repairs, manage contractors |
| Tenant relations | Handle queries, renewals, issues |
| Reporting | Monthly statements, annual tax summaries |

**Fee:** Percentage of monthly rent (TBD)

### 4. Owner Dashboard

Technology platform giving owners visibility into their portfolio.

- Portfolio overview (all properties)
- Income and expense tracking
- Performance against projections
- Tax-ready documentation
- Maintenance history

## Revenue Model

```
┌──────────────────┐     ┌──────────────────┐     ┌──────────────────┐
│  ONCE-OFF FEES   │     │  RECURRING FEES  │     │  PASS-THROUGH    │
├──────────────────┤     ├──────────────────┤     ├──────────────────┤
│ • Sourcing fee   │     │ • Management fee │     │ • Maintenance    │
│ • Placement fee  │     │   (monthly)      │     │   markup         │
│ • Renewal fee    │     │                  │     │                  │
└──────────────────┘     └──────────────────┘     └──────────────────┘
         │                        │                        │
         └────────────────────────┼────────────────────────┘
                                  ▼
                    ┌──────────────────────────┐
                    │   SUSTAINABLE REVENUE    │
                    │   High customer LTV      │
                    │   Recurring + once-off   │
                    └──────────────────────────┘
```

**Key insight:** Management fees create recurring revenue. The more properties under management, the more predictable the income. Once-off fees (sourcing, placement) provide cash flow spikes.

## Competitive Position

| Player | What They Do | Gap We Fill |
|--------|--------------|-------------|
| Estate agents | Sell, then disappear | Ongoing relationship |
| Property managers | Manage, don't source | End-to-end service |
| EasyProperties | Fractional only | Full ownership option |
| Airbnb | Platform only | Full service management |
| DIY | Fragmented, hard work | Turnkey solution |

**Positioning:** The only platform where you can own property (fully or fractionally) AND have it fully managed — from purchase to passive income.
